# Intro
LiveStreamify is an ultra-low-data, audio-first SaaS marketplace for the African market. It allows "Experts" (Tipsters, Lawyers, Mentors) to host paid audio rooms where "Listeners" pay per-minute or per-session via Mobile Money M-Pesa.

Primary Goal: Enable high-value knowledge exchange on 2G/3G networks.

The Problem: Zoom/Teams are too data-heavy and lack integrated micro-billing for the unbanked.

The Pivot: Moving from consuming sports content to monetizing sports/professional expertise.
